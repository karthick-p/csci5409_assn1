CALCULATE BMI :

Body Mass Index(BMI) is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is universally expressed in units of kg/m2, resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres.

Instructions:

  • Enter your weight in kilograms and height in metres
  • Press calculate for the result


BMI Categories: Underweight = <18.5, Normal weight = 18.5–24.9, Overweight = 25–29.9, Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater.
